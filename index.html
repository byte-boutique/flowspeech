How FlowSpeech Works

Our simple process helps you improve your speaking skills in just a few steps.

1

Choose a Topic & Time

Select a speaking topic and set your preferred duration, from 30 seconds to 10 minutes.

2

Record Your Speech

Speak naturally on your chosen topic while the app records your speech with high-quality audio.

3

Get Detailed Feedback

Receive personalized analysis of your speech patterns and topic relevance with actionable suggestions for improvement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FlowSpeech.

FlowSpeech uses advanced speech recognition and NLP technologies to provide highly accurate analysis. Our algorithms have been trained on diverse speech patterns and can identify even subtle instances of repetition, hesitation, filler words, and off-topic segments with over 95% accuracy.

By default, we store your recordings securely to help track your progress over time. However, you can opt to have recordings automatically deleted after analysis in the privacy settings. All data is encrypted and stored in compliance with privacy regulations.

At launch, FlowSpeech will support English (US, UK, Australia, Canada). We plan to add Spanish, French, German, and Mandarin Chinese in the months following our initial release. Additional languages will be added based on user demand.

The recording functionality works offline, but analysis requires an internet connection to access our speech processing services. We're developing a lightweight offline analysis mode for future releases that will provide basic feedback without connectivity.

We're currently in closed beta testing and plan to launch publicly in Q3 2023. Join our waiting list to be among the first to get access when we launch, with special early adopter pricing and features.
